Section 1 — General Information

10 fields · Who is requesting, what's being purchased, and from whom

Use the "Request Other Purchase" form in Zip

▾
1.1

Requester Name & Email

Enter your full name and work email address. This identifies who initiated the request.

1.2

Department

Enter your department as it appears in Workday (e.g., Engineering, People, Finance).

1.3

Entity (Office)

Select the Amplitude legal entity this contract is for.

Amplitude Inc. (US) Amplitude Analytics B.V. (NL) Amplitude Analytics GmbH (DE) Amplitude Analytics Ltd. (UK) Amplitude Analytics G.K. (JP) Amplitude Analytics Pte. Ltd (SG) Amplitude Analytics SAS (FR) Amplitude Canada Limited Amplitude Analytics Australia Pty Ltd
1.4

Contract Type

Select the type that best fits your engagement. Choose from the three options below:

1 · Staffing Agency Services (most common)
Use when you either (a) already have a vendor identified, or (b) need to select a vendor for contractor support.
2 · Individual Consultant
Use for an individual person (not a company/LLC) who will: (a) appear by name in the signature block, and (b) provide consulting, advising, or content creation services to Amplitude.
3 · Professional Services
Use when the vendor is a company (corporation or LLC) whose company name appears in the signature block, and will provide consulting, advising, or content creation services to Amplitude.
1.5

Request Title

Give your request a descriptive title — this becomes the request ID.

Example: "Data Engineer Contractor for Q3 Migration"

1.6

Description of Services

Provide a detailed description of what this contractor will do — include specific scope, deliverables, and timeline. Vague descriptions get de-prioritized.

Section 4 — Purchase Details

4 fields · Contract price, payment method, and dates

▾
4.1

Total Contract Price

Enter the all-in amount in USD (e.g., $10,000.00).

💡
If you don't have a fixed price, calculate it as: hourly billing rate × total contracted hours (e.g., $75/hr × 100 hrs = $7,500.00).
